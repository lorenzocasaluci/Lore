About Me

A Passionate Educator
Rooted in Language

I am Dr. Edoardo Dezi — a Cambridge Qualified English Teacher and Educational Consultant based in Italy. With a deep passion for language and communication, I have been helping learners of all levels achieve their goals for over a decade.

I hold a university degree from the Università degli Studi 'Gabriele d'Annunzio' di Chieti and am an active academic researcher. My bilingual background gives me a unique insight into how Italian speakers learn English — allowing me to anticipate challenges and tailor lessons accordingly.

Whether you are a student preparing for an exam, a professional looking to sharpen your business English, or someone who simply wants to feel more confident speaking — I design every lesson around your specific needs.
